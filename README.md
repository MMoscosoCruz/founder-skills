# Founder Skills

High-signal Claude Code skills for early-stage founders who need to cut through conflicting startup advice and make better decisions.

Founder Skills is a small, opinionated skill library for founders working through the messy earliest stages of company-building: validating ideas, avoiding overbuilding, handling conflicting advice, preparing for investor conversations, and deciding what to do next.

The goal is not to collect generic startup advice. The goal is to turn strong founder judgment into reusable AI skills.

## Who this is for

Founder Skills is for:

- First-time founders who are unsure whether their idea is worth pursuing
- Early-stage founders getting conflicting advice from investors, advisors, accelerators, or other founders
- Founders preparing for pre-seed or seed investor conversations
- Builders who want to test demand before overbuilding
- Startup advisors who want a sharper way to challenge founder thinking

## What this repo helps with

This repo helps founders answer questions like:

- Is this idea actually worth building?
- Am I confusing interest with real customer pain?
- What is the smallest useful version I should build?
- Should I listen to this investor, advisor, accelerator, or founder?
- Am I ready to raise, or should I get more customer evidence first?
- What should I do next this week?

## Available skills

### `validate-idea`

Use this when you have a startup idea but do not yet know whether the problem is painful, frequent, urgent, and specific enough.

It helps you clarify:

- Target customer
- Current workaround
- Pain intensity
- Evidence quality
- Biggest assumptions
- Next validation steps
- Whether to build, validate more, reposition, or kill the idea

### `build-mvp`

Use this when you are tempted to build too much too early.

It helps you decide:

- What assumption the MVP must test
- What can be faked manually
- What should not be built yet
- What the first usable version should include
- How to get the product in front of real users quickly

### `evaluate-advice`

Use this when different people are giving you conflicting advice.

It helps you compare advice based on:

- Who the advice is optimized for
- What stage the advice assumes
- Whether it pushes you toward customer evidence or startup theater
- Whether it comes from pattern recognition or personal preference
- What action you should take next

### `pre-seed-pitch`

Use this when preparing for investor meetings, accelerator interviews, or fundraising conversations.

It helps you sharpen:

- Why this problem
- Why now
- Why you
- What evidence you already have
- What investors are likely to question
- What to fix before pitching harder

## Recommended workflow

Start with the skill that matches your current situation:

```txt
I have an idea, but I am not sure it is good
→ use validate-idea

I know the problem, but I am unsure what to build first
→ use build-mvp

I am getting conflicting advice
→ use evaluate-advice

I am preparing for investors
→ use pre-seed-pitch
